1. Introduction

Welcome to IP Tools : Network Scanner ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our app handles your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data when you use our network scanning application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Network Information

Our app accesses the following network-related information to provide its core functionality:

WiFi Network Information

We access your WiFi network name (SSID), IP address, gateway information, and network configuration to identify and scan devices on your local network.

Device Discovery Data

We scan for devices connected to your local network, including IP addresses, MAC addresses, device names, and open ports for network analysis purposes.

Location Information

We request location permission as required by Android to access WiFi network information. We do not collect, store, or transmit your precise location data.

2.2 Local Storage Data

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Network scan results and device information
  • Router history and previously connected networks
  • App preferences and settings
  • Theme preferences and user interface settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Network Analysis: To scan and identify devices on your local network
  • Device Discovery: To provide detailed information about connected devices
  • Port Scanning: To analyze open ports and services on network devices
  • Network History: To maintain a history of previously scanned networks
  • App Functionality: To provide core features and maintain user preferences

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data collected by our app is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or share any network information, scan results, or personal data with external servers, third parties, or our own systems.

4.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data:

  • Data is stored using secure local storage mechanisms
  • No network transmission of sensitive information
  • App permissions are used only for their intended purposes
  • Regular security updates and improvements

5. Permissions Explained

Our app requests the following permissions and explains why each is necessary:

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION

Required by Android to access WiFi network information. We do not use this permission to track your location.

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE

Allows the app to view information about WiFi networking, such as whether WiFi is enabled and the name of connected WiFi devices.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Allows the app to view information about network connections, such as which networks exist and are connected.

INTERNET

Required for network scanning functionality and accessing network resources on your local network.

CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE

Allows the app to enter WiFi multicast mode for advanced network discovery features.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data to third parties. All network scanning and analysis is performed locally on your device. The app includes a sharing feature that allows you to export scan results, but this is entirely under your control and initiated by you.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained locally on your device according to your usage patterns:

  • Network scan results are stored until you manually delete them
  • Router history is maintained for your convenience
  • App preferences persist until you reset them or uninstall the app
  • All data is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app interface
  • Deletion: You can delete scan results and router history at any time
  • Export: You can export scan results using the built-in sharing feature
  • Permissions: You can revoke app permissions through your device settings
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications.

11. Technical Information

Our app uses standard networking protocols and libraries for network discovery and analysis. All scanning is performed using legitimate network tools and follows industry best practices for network analysis applications.
